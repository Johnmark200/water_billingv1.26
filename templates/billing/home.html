Tabuan Water Billing System

Tabuan, Bayawan City, Negros Oriental, Philippines

Reliable Water Billing, Reading, and Collection Tracking

The Tabuan Water Billing System is a role-based web platform that centralizes water billing operations for administrators, secretaries, treasurers, readers, and consumers. It manages core records such as consumer profiles, meter readings, billing statements, payments, notifications, audit logs, and meeting minutes in one system. Each user role is given access only to the tools relevant to their responsibilities, which helps keep the workflow organized and secure.

Operationally, the system supports the full billing cycle from meter reading submission to billing generation, payment tracking, receipt handling, and reporting. Secretaries can prepare structured meeting minutes with revision tracking, approval locking, PDF export, and a clean toggle-based editing interface, while admins can monitor those minutes through both the admin dashboard and Django admin.

From a management and oversight perspective, the system is designed to improve accuracy, traceability, and coordination across office functions. Real-time dashboard summaries help staff monitor pending bills, overdue accounts, payments, and system activity, while built-in reporting and approval flows reduce manual tracking work across the association.
