Tabuan Water Billing System

Tabuan, Bayawan City, Negros Oriental, Philippines

Reliable Water Billing, Reading, and Collection Tracking

The Tabuan Water Billing System is a role-based web platform that centralizes water billing operations for administrators, secretaries, treasurers, readers, and consumers. It manages core records such as consumer profiles, meter readings, billing statements, payments, notifications, audit logs, and meeting minutes in one system.

Operationally, the system supports the full billing cycle from meter reading submission to billing generation, payment tracking, receipt handling, and reporting. Real-time dashboard summaries help staff monitor pending bills, overdue accounts, payments, and system activity while consumers get direct visibility into their balances and notifications.

The interface is organized by role so each user sees only the tools relevant to their work, improving accuracy, traceability, and coordination across the association.
