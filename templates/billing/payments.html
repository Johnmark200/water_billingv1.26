Payment Processing Guide

How to Process Online Payments with PayMongo

Quick Steps:
  1. Select Consumer: Choose the consumer from the dropdown list
  2. Choose Billing Record: Select the appropriate billing record (filtered by consumer)
  3. Select Payment Method: Choose either Cash or Online Payment
  4. Choose E-Wallet: If online is selected, pick GCash or Maya in the modal
  5. Fill Payment Details: Review amount, status, and optional reference number
  6. Save Payment: Submit the cash or online payment record
Important Tips:
  • Use Cash for over-the-counter settlements that do not need wallet selection
  • Use GCash or Maya only when recording PayMongo e-wallet transactions
  • Save payment reference numbers after completion when one is available
